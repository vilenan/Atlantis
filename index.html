the highlights of atlantis

Maria Jones, a reporter for the real estate magazine “House”, asked Alex Ray, Atlantis' chief architect, about the apartment complex, it’s features, apartment differences, and more.

Atlantis amazes with it’s grandeur and uniqueness of architecture. But what is behind the exterior? How is Atlantis different from other residential complexes?

Of course, when designing a building, we thought about how it will decorate the city, combine with the area. But much more important for us is the comfort of apartments, the usability of space. Residences in Atlantis are built on a white box fine finishes including precious wood parquet, built-in light, Italian marble floors, unique kitchens, and bathrooms. The planning of the residences in the Atlantis is absolutely correct, observing the axial structure and classical principles, with traditional zoning. They have a very clean and clear structure, so they are easy to furnish. We’re sure nobody wants to break walls change the layout of apartments.

The residential complex is located in the center of the city, which means that the infrastructure is very developed here. What other features does Atlantis have?

Yes, you're right about the infrastructure. A 10-minute walk from the complex is a prestigious lyceum, University is also very close. But despite the fact that around a bustling city living a busy life, Atlantis is quiet and calm. The complex has a huge territory with ponds, gardens, children's and sports grounds. Atlantis is like a small city with it’s own life.

Atlantis is like a small city with it’s own life

Do the apartments have a common style? How do apartments differ from each other?

There are 4 types of apartments in Atlantis. They share a common style, according to the Atlantis philosophy, they are sleek, minimalist and modern. But at the same time, they differ from each other. First, they have different areas, from 90 to 230 sq.m. The layouts are also different, designed for different purposes and apartment residents. For example, apartments of the "Loft" the "Family" category are designed so that they will be most comfortable for families with children.
